Mitosis Game

Complete each phase of Mitosis to divide the cell successfully.

Interphase

In this phase, the cell prepares for division. Do you want to explore more about interphase?

Prophase

The chromosomes condense. Are you ready to align the chromosomes?

Metaphase

In Metaphase, chromosomes align in the center of the cell. Do you want to align them?

Anaphase

During Anaphase, the chromosomes are pulled apart to opposite sides of the cell. Ready to pull apart?

Telophase

During Telophase, the cell prepares to divide into two cells. Are you ready for cell division?

Cytokinesis

In this final step, the cytoplasm divides, and two new daughter cells are formed. Do you want to complete cell division?
