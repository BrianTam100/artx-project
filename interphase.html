Interphase - Phases Breakdown

G1 Phase: Cell Growth

During the G1 phase, the cell grows in size, produces RNA and proteins, and prepares for DNA replication.

S Phase: DNA Replication

In S phase, DNA is replicated. The entire genome is copied to prepare for cell division.

G2 Phase: Preparing for Mitosis

In the G2 phase, the cell synthesizes more proteins and organelles to prepare for mitosis.
